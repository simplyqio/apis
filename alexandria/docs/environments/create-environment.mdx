Creating a new environment gives you a completely isolated environment for your integrations.

1. Navigate to the [dashboard](https://app.simplyq.io) and click on the **Environments** dropdown as seein in the below screenshot.

<ThemedImage
  alt="environments dropdown"
  class="bordered margin-left--md"
  sources={{
    light: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-dropdown.png'),
    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-dropdown.png'),
  }}
  width="auto"
  height="200px"
/>

<br/>
<br/>
2. Click on the **New Environment** button once you expand the dropdown.
<br/>
<br/>

<ThemedImage
  alt="environments dropdown"
  class="bordered margin-left--md"
  sources={{
    light: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-dropdown-expanded.png'),
    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-dropdown-expanded.png'),
  }}
  width="auto"
  height="280px"
/>

<br/>
<br/>
3. Enter the name, region and environment tag for your new environment, and click on the **Create** button.
<br/>
<br/>

<ThemedImage
  alt="environments dropdown"
  class="bordered margin-left--md"
  sources={{
    light: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-create-form.png'),
    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/environment-create-form.png'),
  }}
  width="auto"
  height="500px"
/>

<br/>
<br/>
4. You will be redirected to the dashboard's home page, and the environment will have been selected as seen in the top left corner of the page.
